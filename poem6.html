a solitary plume overwhelmed,




                                          untouched, that

                        a cap of midnight grazes, or encounters, and fixes,
                        in crumpled velvet with a sombre burst of laughter,

                        that rigid whiteness, derisory, in opposition to the heavens,
                        too much so not to signal closely any bitter prince of the reef,



                                                                                                heroically adorned with it,
                                                                                                indomitable,
                                                                                                

























                                                                                                but contained by his petty reason,
                                                                                                virile in lightning
